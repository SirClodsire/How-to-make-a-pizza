How to make a delicious pizza!

Ever wanted to make a pizza? Here's an EZ recipe!

Ingredients:

2 cups of flour

1 tsp of yeast

60ml of water (~35-40 degrees)

Toppings (As much as you want). Examples include cheese, pepperoni, onions, mushrooms, peppers, and ground beef clusters.

Recipe:

1. Mix flour with dry yeast, sugar, baking powder (optional) and salt.

2. Add water a little at a time until all water has been added. Mix until you see lumps of dough forming.

3. Knead the dough until it is formed into a giant lump of dough. Do not over-knead it, or the texture will become extremely hard.

4. Roll the dough into a flat, circular shape. Don't make it too thick or it will be undercooked, and don't make it too thin either.

5. Spread tomato sauce over the surface, followed by sprinkling mozerilla over it.

6. Put other ingredients you want on your pizza. ex. Pepperoni, mushrooms, spinich, shrimp or anything else you think will taste good on pizza!

7. Bake in a oven preheated to 200c for 12 min (16 if it's stuffed crust)

8. PIZZA
